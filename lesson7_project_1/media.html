Great Websites to learn more about Disney:

Disneyfoodblog This website is all about the different types of food you can find in Disney World. Each quick place and sit down restaraunt have their own unique food. During the Epcot Food and Wine festival people visit just to eat and drink.
ShopDisney This website is a great place to shop for all things Disney. There is exclusive merchandise on this site you can't buy anywhere else. It is a great tool for people visiting Disney to buy things they can't find in stores.
Disney Guide This isn't an official Disney website yet it is extremely reliable and popular. It has all information about the parks and includes information on every Disney park in the world. It includes schedules of show times in the parks and information on food.
Mouse Planet This is a guide of Disney World including history and facts on Disney. It includes articles on movies and locations in the parks.
Movie Club This is a club program you can join to recieve Disney movies monthly. Disney movies are famous and what shapes their parks.
Planning Guide This is the website people use to plan their Disney vacation based on the feedback from the blog. It has information about tickets, hotels, dining and everything else you need to plan.
