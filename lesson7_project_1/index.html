Overview

Welcome to the Sunshine State of Florida!One of the most popular cities for tourists is Orlando because of all the theme parks and entertainment.

The most famous of all is Walt Disney World.It was created in 1965 in Bay Lake Florida. The first theme park to open was Magic Kingdom which is still the most popular park to date.

Some of the top reasons people visit Disney World is because of the famous movies and television shows created from the company. Some of these certain movies and shows are brought to life with the rides and shows at the park.

One major reason: This massive World has 4 theme parks, 2 water parks, twenty seven themed resorts, golf courses and so much more across the 25,000 acres of land it lives on.

Top 5 Reasons to Vacation to Disney:

  1. Vacation Packages
  2. One of a kind dining experiences
  3. Chance to meet your favorite characters
  4. Different activites for all age groups
  5. Shopping District

Out of all the theme parks in the world, Disney World has always remained on top as having the highest attendance of any park on the globe.

Top Park Attendance 2018 (According to Disney News US for March 2018):

  1. Magic Kingdom 20.4 mill
  2. Epcot 12.2 mill
  3. Animal Kingdom 12.5 mill
  4. Hollywood Studios 10.7 mill
